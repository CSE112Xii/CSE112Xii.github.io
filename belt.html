Conveyor Belt

Last updated: June 14th, 2018

Initial Version

This was our initial conveyor belt. We constructed this way because we believed we would be spending majority of our time developing the product, following the Agile methodology. Inspired by the Space Shuttle Article, We wanted to document every step of the way. This is why we placed documentation in between front-end and back-end development as well as for reviews.

Final Version

We decided to change our conveyor belt as we realized there are more things to research for than we had initially anticipated. This new conveyor belt reflects the amount of emphasis our team has put in the planning stage. Notice how we have included more steps. All of these indicate what we have learned from the first seven weeks of this class.
